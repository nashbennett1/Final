Ferrari is an iconic Italian luxury sports car manufacturer renowned for its high-performance vehicles and rich racing heritage. Founded in 1939 by Enzo Ferrari, the company has become synonymous with speed, style, and exclusivity. Ferrari's cars are meticulously engineered and handcrafted, featuring powerful engines, sleek aerodynamic designs, and cutting-edge technologies that deliver unparalleled driving experiences. From the classic 250 series to modern marvels like the LaFerrari and SF90 Stradale, Ferrari's lineup showcases the brand's relentless pursuit of performance, innovation, and automotive excellence. Beyond their on-road prowess, Ferrari's racing pedigree is unmatched, with numerous victories in prestigious events like the 24 Hours of Le Mans and Formula One World Championships. The prancing horse emblem has become a symbol of automotive passion and a coveted status symbol for car enthusiasts and collectors worldwide.
